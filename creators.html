FAQ

You've got
questions.

How exactly do I get paid? +
Each campaign gives you a unique QR code and tracking link. When your followers use it at the venue door, it logs a verified redemption. Once your campaign ends, VYBE pays you via direct deposit or Venmo within 48 hours. No invoices. No chasing.
Do I need a massive following to get accepted? +
No. We have creators with 3,500 followers who out-earn accounts with 70K — because their audience is local and engaged. What matters is: are your followers in Miami/South Florida, and do they actually go out? We check this when you apply.
What kind of content do I have to make? +
Most creators do a TikTok or Reel — something like "going to [Venue] this Friday, use my link for a free drink." Authentic, casual content outperforms polished ads. Each campaign gives you a brief with requirements, but we keep them minimal on purpose.
Does my post have to say it's sponsored? +
Yes — and we make it easy. VYBE auto-generates the correct FTC disclosure for your caption (usually just #ad or #sponsored). This protects you legally. Every brief comes with the exact language you need. Never skip this — it's both a legal requirement and a trust signal for your audience.
Can I run multiple campaigns at once? +
Yes. Once you're approved, you can pick any available campaigns that match your audience profile. Most active creators run 2-4 campaigns per month. Your dashboard shows all active campaigns, redemptions in real time, and pending payouts.
What happens if my followers don't show up? +
You only earn on verified redemptions. If no one uses your code, you earn $0 for that campaign — but there's no cost to you either. This is why we match you to venues that fit your specific audience, so campaigns are more likely to convert.
